# PPG (Photoplethysmography) Technology Research

## What is PPG?
Photoplethysmography is an optical measurement technique that detects blood volume changes in microvascular tissue.

## How Our System Works:
1. IR LED emits light into finger tissue
2. Photodetector measures reflected light
3. Blood pulses cause slight light absorption changes
4. These changes are detected as electrical pulses

## Medical Relevance:
- **Heart Rate**: Pulse detection and BPM calculation
- **Blood Oxygen**: Possible extension with red LED
- **Perfusion**: Tissue blood flow indication
- **Arrhythmia**: Irregular pulse pattern detection

## Accuracy Factors:
1. **Sensor Placement**: Consistent finger positioning
2. **Ambient Light**: Minimize interference
3. **Pressure**: Optimal contact without restricting flow
4. **Motion**: Reduce movement artifacts
